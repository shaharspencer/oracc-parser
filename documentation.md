# Data Notes

## Texts without catalogue metadata

A sanity check was run matching all text files in `enriched_data/oracc_csvs/` against their project's catalogue CSV in `enriched_data/catalogues/`. Out of 40,473 texts checked, 263 texts across 16 projects have no corresponding entry in their project's catalogue.

These are not code bugs — they reflect gaps in the upstream ORACC catalogue data.

For `atae/durkatlimmu`, 198 X-numbered texts are present in the subproject's corpus JSON but are listed only in the parent `atae` project's catalogue, not in the subproject's own `catalogue.json`. This is the biggest amount of documents with metadata. Most if not all of these texts are duplicates of P- numbered texts in `atae/durkatlimmu`, which have a catalogue entry. They were not removed en masse because we have yet to validate that all 198 texts are duplicates of P- numbered texts.
